### For Reporters/Victims:
- **Anonymous Reporting**: Option to submit reports anonymously or with identification
- **Secure Platform**: End-to-end encryption to protect sensitive information
- **Case Tracking**: Monitor the status of submitted reports
- **Support Resources**: Access to counseling and support information

- **Document Management**: Store and organize case-related documents
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Generate insights and reports on harassment trends

- Data encryption at rest and in transit
- Audit logging for all system activities
