# Campus Safety - Sexual Harassment Management Application

A mobile application designed to help manage, report, and track sexual harassment cases on campus. This application provides a safe, confidential, and efficient platform for victims, administrators, and support staff to handle sensitive incidents with care and professionalism.

## üì± Overview

Campus Safety is a comprehensive mobile solution that empowers individuals to report sexual harassment incidents while providing administrators with the tools to effectively manage, investigate, and resolve cases. The application prioritizes user privacy, data security, and a trauma-informed approach to case management.

## ‚ú® Key Features

### For Victims/Reporters
- **Anonymous Reporting**: Submit reports with full anonymity or choose to identify yourself
- **Secure Communication**: End-to-end encrypted messaging with case handlers
- **Case Tracking**: Monitor the status and progress of your report in real-time
- **Evidence Upload**: Safely upload photos, videos, documents, and audio recordings
- **Resource Center**: Access support resources, counseling services, and legal information
- **Emergency Contacts**: Quick access to campus security, police, and crisis hotlines

### For Administrators
- **Case Management Dashboard**: Centralized view of all reported cases
- **Investigation Tools**: Document findings, interview notes, and evidence collection
- **Status Updates**: Keep reporters informed about case progress
- **Analytics & Reporting**: Generate insights on incident patterns and trends
- **User Management**: Manage roles and permissions for staff members
- **Compliance Tools**: Ensure adherence to Title IX and other regulations

### For Support Staff
- **Case Assignment**: Receive and manage assigned cases
- **Communication Hub**: Securely communicate with reporters and other staff
- **Documentation**: Maintain detailed case notes and action logs
- **Referral System**: Connect victims with appropriate support services

## üîí Security & Privacy

- **End-to-End Encryption**: All communications and data are encrypted
- **Secure Data Storage**: Compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, FERPA)
- **Access Controls**: Role-based permissions ensure data is only accessible to authorized personnel
- **Anonymous Reporting**: IP addresses and device information are not logged for anonymous reports
- **Audit Trails**: All actions are logged for accountability and compliance

## üì± Usage

### Reporting an Incident
1. Open the application
2. Choose "Report an Incident"
3. Select whether to report anonymously or with identification
4. Fill in the incident details (date, time, location, description)
5. Upload any supporting evidence (optional)
6. Submit the report
7. Receive a unique case ID for tracking

### Tracking a Case
1. Navigate to "My Cases" or "Track Case"
2. Enter your case ID or view your submitted cases
3. Check status updates and messages from administrators

### Managing Cases (Admin)
1. Log in with administrator credentials
2. View dashboard with all active cases
3. Assign cases to investigators
4. Update case status and communicate with reporters
5. Generate reports and analytics
